ECHTNICE STUDIO

ECHTNICE is a game studio based in Dortmund, Germany. ECHTNICE was founded in early 2018 by Paul Mizel and Fabian Raetz. We create different games and attach great importance to the best gaming experience, whether for PC or smartphone.

With the flexibility and willingness to adapt to the market, you never know what the next ECHTNICE game could be - but you can be sure that it will blow your mind. Because at ECHTNICE we breathe games.

They are now working hard to grow ECHTNICE Studios into an established games company.
